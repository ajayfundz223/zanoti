We have a diverse clientele and love the opportunity to work with anybody interested in improving: beginner to advanced, seniors, youth, and everything in between. A large percentage our clients are beginners age 40-70


The process starts by meeting at the gym for an introductory dicussion Q&A. Whenever you decide to start up, we get you scheduled for a series of planned-progression training sessions. We start with a sensible and light workout to assess your current level of strength, conditioning, and flexibility.

From there on out your training is a constant adaptation of progressive improvements followed by reassesment and program modification.


Availability is subject to each trainer's schedule, but overall we have time slots in the morning, afternoon, evenings and weekends.
